Our Journey of Compassion

For over 15 years, Prem Foundation has been transforming lives through sustainable community initiatives.

Milestones of Impact

2008

Foundation Established

Founded by social entrepreneurs to address education inequality in rural Maharashtra.

2012

First Community Center

Opened our flagship community center serving 200+ families with vocational training.

2015

Premangan Inauguration

Launched our elder care facility with 30 beds and holistic wellness programs.

2020

Pandemic Response

Distributed 25,000+ meal kits and organized mobile health camps across 12 villages.
