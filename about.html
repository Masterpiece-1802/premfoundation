Our Journey of Compassion

For over 15 years, Prem Foundation has been at the forefront of transformative change, pioneering sustainable community initiatives that have touched thousands of lives across Maharashtra. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has established us as a beacon of hope and progress in the social sector.

Milestones of Impact

2008

Foundation Established

Founded with a vision to create sustainable change in rural Maharashtra through education and empowerment initiatives.

2012

First Community Center

Opened our flagship center providing vocational training, education programs, and healthcare services to 200+ families.

2015

Premangan Inauguration

Launched our state-of-the-art elder care facility offering holistic wellness programs and compassionate care.

2020

Pandemic Response

Mobilized resources to distribute 25,000+ meal kits and organize mobile health camps across 12 villages during COVID-19.
