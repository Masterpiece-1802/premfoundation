Our Journey of Compassion

For over 13 years, Prem Foundation (Sister organisation of Baglan Seva Samiti) has been at the forefront of transformative change, pioneering sustainable community initiatives that have touched thousands of lives across Maharashtra. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has established us as a beacon of hope and progress in the social sector.

Our Core Values

Compassion

Every initiative begins with deep empathy and respect for those we serve, ensuring dignity in all our interactions and programs.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of transparency and ethical conduct, with unwavering accountability to our stakeholders.

Innovation

We constantly push boundaries to develop creative, sustainable solutions that maximize our impact and reach.
