About Prem Foundation

Committed to dignity, care, and community service

Our Mission

To provide compassionate care and sustainable solutions for the elderly and underserved communities through holistic programs.

Our Vision

A society where every individual ages with dignity, and no one is left behind due to socioeconomic barriers.

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, Prem Foundation began with a small shelter for homeless elders and has since expanded to multiple initiatives including healthcare, education, and community development.
