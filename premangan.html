About Premangan

Providing compassionate elderly care since 2015

Premangan offers a safe, nurturing environment with 24/7 professional care, nutritious meals, and engaging activities tailored for seniors.

  • 50+ residents cared for
  • 15 trained caregivers
  • 5 acres of peaceful gardens
